Data Preparation and Preprocessing

Data Preparation

Data preparation and preprocessing are important steps in data analysis and machine learning. Data preparation involves collecting and cleaning data by handling missing values, removing duplicates, and ensuring consistency. Preprocessing further improves the data by scaling, normalizing, and selecting relevant features. These steps make the data clean, accurate, and ready for building better models.

Data Preprocessing in Our Data

  • Unnecessary attributes containing 100% NULL values were removed. A total of 18 attributes that contained 100% NULL values were dropped as they did not provide any useful data for analysis.
  • Attributes containing redundant information were identified and removed.11 such attributes were eliminated to reduce noise and improve data quality.
  • In the ‘Value’ attribute, entries like "Elimination Verified" or "No PC Required" were assigned a value of zero,as it reflects regions where treatment was no longer needed and no further programmatic action was required.
  • The ‘Value’ attribute was initially in String format and then converted to Numerical format,since it primarily contained numerical values and needed to be processed mathematically in the analysis.
  • The skewness of the ‘Value’ attribute was checked before handling missing values,to choose an appropriate imputation method that maintains the integrity of the data distribution.
  • Used median filling method to fill the missing values in the ‘Value’ attribute,as the median is more robust to outliers and better suited for skewed data compared to the mean.
  • To ensure data consistency and maintain accuracy, the median imputation method was applied separately for each region,so that the specific characteristics of each region were preserved during the data cleaning process.
  • This approach helped maintain the integrity of the dataset,ensuring that preprocessing did not distort regional patterns or introduce bias in further analysis.
  • Before Preprocessing: 681 tuples and 34 attributes.
  • After Preprocessing: 681 tuples and 5 attributes.
